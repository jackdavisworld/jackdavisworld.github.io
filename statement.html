STATEMENT

I am an artist, designer, writer, and journalist: a multidisciplinary creator.

I explore identity through both individuality and collectivity in a digitizing society.

I engage in processes both two- and three-dimensional, including garment construction, digital imaging, and mixed-media.

I work conceptually, iteratively, and experimentally.

I find inspiration from literature, history, and science. I have created in direct response to works by individuals such as Euripides, Sigmund Freud, and Don DeLillo.

I study at The New School in New York City, where I pursue a BFA in fashion design from Parsons School of Design and a BA in journalism from Eugene Lang College of Liberal Arts.
